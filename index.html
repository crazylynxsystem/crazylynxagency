ABOUT US AND
OUR PHILOSOPHY


We are a dedicated team of professionals ready to help your business grow by building your online presence through a solid website and digital marketing.

Our philosophy is to bring the highest possible value to our clients. In line with this, we only accept engagements that potentially show positive return on investment. We love our reputation far more than revenue.

If your engagement proposal happens to get rejected, it could only mean three things:

First, your business doesn't necessarily need our service. Your cost might far surpass your benefits.

Second, based on our assessment you lack information resulting to hasty decisions. We don't want to take advantage of that. In fact, we will provide you with necessary resources you could learn from for free. It's up to you if you still want our service or not. Helping our clients make sound decisions makes us happy.

Third, we don't have experience in working with your business model. However, if you insist, we might accept your engagement proposal and apply what works with other business models.

We only want the best for you. To provide you the perfect web and digital marketing solution, we need to understand your business well.

So we created a blueprint in your road to growth and success. It consists three steps.

Steps for Growing Your Business

This section of our website discusses the steps in our client acceptance procedure.

  • Step 1
  • Step 2
  • Step 3

  • HELP US UNDERSTAND YOUR BUSINESS

    Through this way, we will have enough information on planning the right approach in your website and digital marketing needs

  • PRE-ENGAGEMENT FORM

    You will find below a pre-engagement form. It will ask you basic details about you and your business.

  • CONFIRMATION AND FURTHER DETAILS

    We will email you within 24hrs after we review your form. We may ask further questions if necessary. This will ultimately help us decide whether we could work together or not.

  • WORK WITH US

    If we decided to accept your application, we will again email you. Then you may set an appointment schedule by contacting us. If it is geographically impossible, we may meet via video call.

  • WE ARE NOW READY FOR THE NEXT STEP

  • WE EXPLAIN IN DETAIL WHAT WE DO

    We will explain to you in detail what will be our role in your journey to growth and success. We will also address all your questions to clarify things up.

  • GENERAL MARKETING STRATEGIES

    We will discuss to you the general marketing strategies that works on the same type of business as you do. Take note, this general strategy may not work for your business. But stay calm as we will soon draft a customized strategy just for you.

  • DETAILS ON COST

    You only pay for our professional service. It doesn't include the cost of advertising in different media. We will explain to you in detail the average cost of different advertising media.

  • CONTRACT SIGNING

    You can still withdraw from the engagement until this point. Once we sign the contract, we are now both committed to our respected responsibilities.

  • WE ARE NOW READY FOR THE FINAL STEP

  • EMAIL ON PAYMENT GATEWAY

    After contract signing, we will email you the payment gateway. We usually use Paypal as this is secure and buyer friendly. This will trigger the commencement of our work.

  • INITIAL OBJECTIVES SETTING

    We will lay down initial objectives that is necessary for measuring future success. These objectives may change over time.

  • MARKETING AND ACTION PLAN

    Depending on how fast information can be gathered on your business, it will take 2 to 5 weeks time to draft the marketing and action plan specifically made for your business.

  • COMMENCEMENT OF MARKETING AND ACTION PLAN

    This is now the real deal. A plan is just a plan if not properly executed. Through our team, we will make the plan possible and produce above-average results.
